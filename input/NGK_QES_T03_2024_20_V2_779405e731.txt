1.Introduction
Giói thièu
This Qualifying Explanatory Statement (QES) contains all the required information on the carbon
neutrality of the given subject. All information provided within this report will be reviewed by a third
party and is believed to be correct. If provided with any information affecting the validity of the
following statements, this document will be updated accordingly to reflect Vietnam Beverage
Factory's current status towards carbon neutrality.
Tuyèn bó Giai thích Dú dièu kien (QES) này chúra tát ca các thòng tin càn thiét vè carbon trung tính
cúa dói tuong nhát dinh. Tát cá thòng tin duo'c cung cáp trong báo cáo này sē duoc xem xét vè tính
dúng dán bói bēn thúr 3. Néu duoc cung cáp bát ky thòng tin nào ánh huóng dén tính hgp lè cúa
các tuyèn bó sau dày, tài liēu này sē dugc càp nhàt tuong úng dě phán ánh tinh trang hiēn tai cúa
Nhà máy Nuóc Giai Khát VN aói vói carbon trung tiính.
This report will be made publicly available on Vinamilk's webpage: https://www.vinamilk.com.vn
Tuyèn bó này sě duoc còng bó trèn trang web cúa Vinamilk: https://www.vinamilk.com.vn
Based on the roadmap of Vietnam Dairy Products Joint Stock Company on business development
associated with sustainable development, the Company currently focuses on neutralizing direct
GHG emissions and GHG emissions from importing energy. Therefore, the factory chooses to be
carbon neutral from Scope 1 and 2 emission sources for this period. This is Vietnam Beverage
Factory's first declaration of commitment to carbon neutrality for scope 1 and scope 2 GHG
emissions.
Dya vào lo trinh cúa Còng ty CP Süa Viet Nam vè viec phát trién kinh doanh gán lièn vói phát trién
bèn vǔng, hiēn tai Còng ty tàp trung vào vièc trung hòa nguòn phát thai KNK tryc tiép và nguòn phát
thai KNK tùr vièc nhàp nǎng luong. Do dó, Nhà máy chon trung hòa Carbon các nguòn phát thai
thuòc Scope 1 và 2 cho giai doan này. Dày là tuyèn bó đàu tiēn cúa Nhà máy Nuóc Giai Khát VN
vè cam két trung tính carbon dói vói phát thai KNK pham vi 1 và pham vi 2.
Vietnam Beverage Factory's carbon neutrality declaration will be reviewed and verified by an
independent third party, BSI.

Vietnam Beverage Factory assumes responsibility for its production activities and for the

Vietnam Beverage Factory will put effort into mitigating climatic change.

Vietnam Beverage Factory will reduce its annual emissions of greenhouse gases and
will act responsibly to maintain the climate. In so doing, the factory contributes to
reducing its factory carbon footprint.

Vietnam Beverage Factory will look for solutions, which will lead to sensible

ho'p ly nhàm giam phát thai khí nhà kính.
Vietnam Beverage Factory will maintain a system for the annual monitoring and
reporting of greenhouse gas emissions. This system must be stable, precise,

Nhà máy Nuóc Giai Khát VN sē duy tri hè thóng giám sát và báo cáo phát thai khí

Vietnam Beverage Factory will strive to raise a positive attitude towards the
