3. Hoat dong giám sát cua HDQT döi vói Ban Giám döc/Supervising the Board of Management by the Board of Directors:
Cǎn cúr Diěu le Còng ty và Quy che noi bo vě Quan tri Cong ty, Hoi dǒng Quan tri da thuc hien viec kiem tra, giám sát hoat
dòng cúa Töng Giám döc ("TGD") và Ban Dieu hành trong viec diěu hành san xuat kinh doanh và trien khai các nghi quyet cúa
DHDCD và HDQT trong nǎm 2022 nhu sau:/ Pursuant to the Company's charter, the Internal Corporate governance Statues, the
Board has conducted its role of supervision to the CEO's and the Board of Management in daily operations and implementation
of the AGM's and Board's resolutions in 2022 as below:
2.1 Phuong thúrc giám sát: HDQT thòng qua các tieu ban các nhiem vy cu the duoc dě càp trong Quy chě noi bò vě quán tri
còng ty thyc hièn viec giám sát Töng giám döc và Ban diěu hành to chúc, trien khai và thyc hien các nghi quyět cúa DHDCD,
HDQT. Các chú tich úy ban sě dua ra các noi dung mà Ban diěu hành phai chuan bi de trinh bày/tháo luàn/chat vǎn tai cuòc
hop HDQT. Tai các cuoc hop, HDQT soát xét tinh hinh thyc hien các nghi quyet, tinh hinh SXKD cúa quy và lüy kě, thao luan và
quyět dinh thòng qua các nghi quyět de Töng Giám döc và Ban Diěu hành làm co so trien khai thyc hièn. Ngoài ra, các Uy ban
cúa HDQT cǔng trao doi vói thành vièn Ban dieu hành vě các nòi dung, vǎn dě quan tam./ Supervisory method: through activities
of the Committees with detailed duties set out in the Internal Corporate Governance Statutes, the Board has supervised the
Management Board in the implementation of the AGM and Board's resolutions. The chairmen of the committees gave requests to
the Management Board to prepare contents for presentation/discussion in the board meetings. At all Board meetings, the Board
always reviewed the implementation of the Board's resolutions as well as the performance of the quarter and year-to-date,
discussed and approved resolutions for CEO and the Board of Management to implement them accordingly. In addition, the Board
committee usually discussed with the management interested matters under supervision.
2.2. Kět quá giám sát: Vói nhüng kět quá dat duoc trong nǎm 2022, trong tinh hinh bi anh huóng cúa hàu dai dich Covid-
19, biěn dòng kinh tě chính tri trèn thě giói và xung dot o chàu Au, HDQT da ghi nhàn TGD và Ban Dieu hành da nǒ lyc de vuot
qua các khó khǎn anh huóng děn hoat dòng san xuat kinh doanh, tuàn thú các quy dinh pháp luàt hièn hành và quy chě hoat
dòng cua Còng ty./ Supervisory results: With the results achieved in 2022, in the situation affected by the post-Covid-19 pandemic,
political and economic fluctuations in the world and conflicts in Europe, the Board of Directors recognized that the CEO and the
Board of Management had make efforts to overcome difficulties affecting production and business activities, complying with
current laws and operating regulations of the Company.

the term 2022 - 2026 for the Board of Directors to consider and submit to the 2022 Annual General Meeting of
Shareholders. The NC also reviewed, evaluated and submitted to the Board of Directors for re-appointment of the CEO
for the term 2022 - 2026. Besides, the NC also discussed, developed and completed the Regulation on human resource
development and succession plan for key positions of Vietnam Dairy Products Joint Stock Company (for positions
appointed by the Board of Directors). The Board of Directors, after considering it, have approved this Regulation.

quan ly rúi ro nhàm thuc hièn hieu quá và kip thòi vièc soát xét báo cáo tài chính và tinh hinh tài chính, xem xét dánh

management in order to effectively and promptly reviewed the financial statements and financial situation, reviewed
and assessed risks in operation and prevention methods, ensuring compliance with regulations and procedures of the
Company.
