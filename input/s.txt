RESOLUTION
Ref: officially appointing Mr. Doan Quoc Khanh to the position of
Executive Director of Raw Material Development
BOARD OF DIRECTORS OF VIETNAM DAIRY PRODUCTS JSC
Pursuant to the Law on Enterprises No. 59/2020/QH14 approved by the National Assembly on
June 17, 2020 and guiding documents;
Pursuant to the current Charter of Vietnam Dairy Products Joint Stock Company approved by
the General Meeting of Shareholders on 25/04/2023;
Pursuant to the Record of vote counting for opinions in writing No. 13/BBKP-CTS. Board of
Directors/2024 dated 23/12/2024 of Vietnam Dairy Products Joint Stock Company.
The Board of Directors of Vietnam Dairy Products Joint Stock Company (hereinafter
referred to as "Vinamilk" or the "Company") has approved this Resolution with the
following contents:
RESOLUTION
Article 1: The Board of Directors approved the official appointment of Mr. Doan Quoc
Khanh to the position of Executive Director of Raw Material Development from 1/1/2025.
Article 2: The Board of Directors assigns the CEO to decide on the salary & income
applied to Mr. Doan Quoc Khanh in the income bracket of the approved Executive
Directors in 2023 (according to Resolution No. 20/NQ-CTS.HDQT/2023 of the Board of
Directors dated 25/9/2023).
Article 3: This Resolution takes effect from the date of signing.
The Board of Directors, the CEO, and relevant individuals of Vietnam Dairy Products Joint
Stock Company shall be responsible for the implementation of this Resolution.
