1. Introduction - Giói thieu
This Qualifying Explanatory Statement (QES) contains all the required carbon neutrality
information of the given subject. All information provided within this report will be
reviewed to ensure the accuracy by a third party. Being provided with any information that
can affect the following statements validity would resulted in the action of accordingly
updating this document in order to reflect Vietnam Dairy Factory's current status towards
carbon neutrality.

trung tính cúa dói trong nhát dinh. Tát cá thòng tin duoc cung cáp trong báo cáo này sě
duoc xem xét vè tính dúng dán bói bèn thú 3. Néu duoc cung cáp bát ky thòng tin nào ánh

recalculation investigation, and determine if the base year has changed, and make a new
