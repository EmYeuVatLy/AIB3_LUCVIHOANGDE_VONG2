1. Introduction
Giói thiēu
This Qualifying Explanatory Statement (QES) contains all the required information on the carbon
neutrality of the given subject. Allinformation provided within this report will be reviewed by a third
party and is believed to be correct. If provided with any information affecting the validity of the
following statements, this document will be updated accordingly to reflect Nghe An Dairy Factory
current status towards carbon neutrality.

tính cúa dói tuong nhát dinh. Tát ca thòng tin duoc cung cáp trong báo cáo này sě dugc xem xét
vè tính dúng dán bói bèn thú 3. Néu duoc cung cáp bát ky thòng tin nào ánh huóng dén tính
