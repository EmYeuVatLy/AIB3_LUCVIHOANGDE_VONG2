cáo tài chính gàn nhát cua Còng Ty.

RESOLUTION
Article 1: The Board of Directors approves contracts and transactions between Vinamilk with: (i)
Vinamilk's insiders; (ii) Related persons of Vinamilk's insiders; and (iii) Related persons
of Vinamilk, in which these contracts and transactions are executed on the basis of
conformity with the Company's Charter and relevant laws, ensuring the interests of the
Company and the interests of shareholders. Specifically, as follows:
A detailed list of transactions is listed in the attached Appendix.
The content and terms of the contract/agreement between the Company and the
Related Persons listed in the attached Appendix must ensure fairness according to
market conditions.
Transaction value: as actually incurred, however, it must be ensured that the value of
the contract/transaction must be less than twenty percent (20%) of the total asset
value recorded in the Company's latest financial statements.
Effective period: from 01/01/2024 to 31/12/2025.
Article 2: Assign the CEO, based on the actual needs and operation of the company, to decide on
the detailed contents of contracts between the Company and related persons, and at the
same time organize the implementation and implementation of specific

transactions/contracts to serve production activities, business of the Company, and must
ensure that these Contracts/Transactions meet the guidelines approved by the Board of
Directors in Article 1 of this Resolution and other internal regulations of the Board of
Directors.
Article 3: This Resolution takes effect from the date of signing.
The Board of Directors, the CEO, the Management and relevant departments of Vietnam
Dairy Products Joint Stock Company shall be responsible for the implementation of this
Resolution.
